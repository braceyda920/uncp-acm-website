About Us

Our Chapter, Our Community

Dedicated to advancing computing knowledge and fostering a vibrant tech community at UNCP since 2024.

Our Mission

Computing for Everyone

The UNCP ACM Chapter is the official student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at the University of North Carolina at Pembroke. We are a student-run organization open to all students who have an interest in computing and technology.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world application — providing resources, connections, and experiences that traditional coursework can't always offer.

Whether you're a Computer Science major, a curious first-year, or a student from any other discipline who loves technology, you'll find a home here.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🌍
Inclusion & Belonging

We welcome students of all backgrounds, majors, and experience levels. Diversity of thought makes us stronger as a community.

🔬
Curiosity & Learning

Technology never stands still, and neither do we. We encourage continuous learning and intellectual exploration.

🤲
Collaboration

The best ideas come from working together. We build community through pair programming, team projects, and peer mentorship.

🚀
Impact & Excellence

We push each other to do great work — in the classroom, in competitions, and in our future careers as computing professionals.
