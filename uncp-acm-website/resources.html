Member Resources

Tools & Learning

A curated collection of the best learning platforms, career tools, scholarships, and communities for computing students.

🐍
CS50 by Harvard
The world's most popular intro to computer science. Free, online, and truly excellent for beginners and experienced programmers alike.
Visit cs50.harvard.edu ↗
🎓
Coursera
University-level courses from top institutions. Many certificates are free to audit, and financial aid is available for all programs.
Visit coursera.org ↗
💡
freeCodeCamp
Completely free, self-paced curriculum covering web development, data analysis, machine learning, and more. Earn verifiable certifications.
Visit freecodecamp.org ↗
📖
MIT OpenCourseWare
Free access to virtually all MIT course materials — including lecture notes, assignments, and exams for CS, math, and engineering courses.
Visit ocw.mit.edu ↗
🧠
Khan Academy
Free computing, math, and programming fundamentals. Perfect for brushing up on discrete math or logic before exams.
Visit khanacademy.org ↗
▶️
The Odin Project
A free open-source curriculum for learning web development from scratch. Project-based and deeply practical.
Visit theodinproject.com ↗
💻
LeetCode
The go-to platform for technical interview preparation. Practice data structures and algorithms problems used in real FAANG interviews.
Visit leetcode.com ↗
🤝
LinkedIn
Build your professional profile, connect with UNCP alumni in tech, and discover internship and full-time opportunities.
Visit linkedin.com ↗
🔍
Handshake
UNCP's official job and internship platform. Thousands of employers specifically recruiting from UNCP and similar institutions.
Visit joinhandshake.com ↗
📄
Resume.io
Build a polished, ATS-friendly resume quickly. Use their templates optimized for technical roles and internship applications.
Visit resume.io ↗
🏢
Glassdoor
Research companies, read real employee reviews, and find salary data for roles you're targeting. Prepare smarter for interviews.
Visit glassdoor.com ↗
🧩
Pramp
Free peer-to-peer mock technical interviews. Practice live coding interviews with other students and get real-time feedback.
Visit pramp.com ↗
🐙
GitHub Student Developer Pack
Free access to 100+ developer tools — including GitHub Pro, JetBrains IDEs, Namecheap domains, and more. Apply with your .edu email.
Visit education.github.com ↗
☁️
AWS Educate
Free cloud computing training and AWS credits for students. Build real cloud projects and earn badges to show employers.
Visit aws.amazon.com/educate ↗
🔷
VS Code
The most popular code editor in the world. Free, extensible, and excellent for every language. Install the Live Share extension for pair programming.
Visit code.visualstudio.com ↗
🚀
Vercel
Deploy web projects instantly for free. Perfect for hosting personal portfolios, React apps, and class projects with a real URL.
Visit vercel.com ↗
🗃️
Supabase
Open-source Firebase alternative with PostgreSQL. Free tier is generous — great for building full-stack apps with auth and a real database.
Visit supabase.com ↗
🎨
Figma
The industry-standard UI/UX design tool. Free for students. Use it to prototype apps before building them or to sharpen your design skills.
Visit figma.com ↗
🏅
ACM / ICPC Scholarship
ACM offers several scholarships for computing students, particularly for those involved in competitions and research activities.
Visit acm.org ↗
💎
Google Scholarships
Google offers the Generation Google Scholarship for women and underrepresented groups in CS. $10,000 award plus mentorship opportunities.
Visit buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com ↗
🌟
Microsoft TEALS Scholarship
Supports students pursuing CS degrees, especially from underrepresented communities. Check eligibility and deadlines each fall.
Visit microsoft.com ↗
🎓
UNCF Scholarships
The United Negro College Fund offers dozens of tech-focused scholarships for students at HBCUs and minority-serving institutions like UNCP.
Visit uncf.org ↗
💰
Fastweb
The largest free scholarship search database. Create a profile and get matched to scholarships you're actually eligible for.
Visit fastweb.com ↗
🏛️
UNCP Financial Aid & Scholarships
UNCP's own scholarship portal — check for department-specific awards in CS and IT that many students overlook.
Visit uncp.edu ↗
💬
UNCP ACM Discord
Join our chapter's Discord server to chat with members, get homework help, share resources, and stay up to date on events.
Get Invite Link →
🧑‍💻
Dev.to
A community of software developers sharing articles, tutorials, and career advice. Great for writing your first tech blog post.
Visit dev.to ↗
🤖
Hacker News
Y Combinator's community for tech and startup news. Read it daily to stay informed about what's happening in the industry.
Visit news.ycombinator.com ↗
📡
Stack Overflow
The world's largest developer Q&A community. Chances are your bug has already been solved here. Also a great place to contribute answers.
Visit stackoverflow.com ↗
🌍
ACM Digital Library
Access to the world's largest computing research database. ACM student members get free access to papers and publications.
Visit dl.acm.org ↗
🐦
CodeNewbie Community
A supportive community for people learning to code. Weekly chats, podcasts, and a friendly space for beginners to ask questions without judgment.
Visit community.codenewbie.org ↗
