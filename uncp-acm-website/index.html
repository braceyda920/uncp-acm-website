What We Do

Built for Students, by Students

We create opportunities for every student at UNCP to grow technically and professionally.

🛠️
Hands-On Workshops

From web development to machine learning, our workshops give you practical skills employers actually want — led by peers and industry guests.

🤝
Industry Networking

Connect with recruiters, alumni, and tech professionals at our speaker series and career events. Your next internship might start here.

⚡
Hackathons & Competitions

Put your skills to the test. Compete in coding challenges, hackathons, and ACM programming contests with teammates from UNCP and beyond.

📚
Study & Resource Sharing

Access curated learning resources, study groups, and mentoring from upperclassmen who've already navigated the courses you're taking.
